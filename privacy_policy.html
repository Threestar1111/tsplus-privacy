Privacy Policy

TS+ · Last updated: May 2026

TS+ is a mobile application for the trades industry, developed by an independent developer. This privacy policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding your data.

Information we collect

When you use TS+, the following information may be collected:

  • Phone number — used for account creation and identity verification
  • Name and profile information — provided by you during setup
  • Location — city and province/state, provided by you for your profile
  • Trade and professional information — provided by you to complete your profile
  • Billing address — provided by you for contract and invoice purposes
  • GST/HST number — optionally provided by tradespeople for invoice generation
  • Contract and project data — created by you within the app
  • Usage data — how you interact with the app, collected by Firebase Analytics

How we use your information

Your information is used solely to operate the TS+ app. Specifically:

  • To create and manage your account
  • To connect you with contractors or tradespeople on the platform
  • To generate contracts and invoices within the app
  • To display your profile to other verified users
  • To send app notifications related to your contracts and activity

Data sharing

Your data is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Data is shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With other TS+ users you connect with, as part of normal app functionality (e.g. your name and trade are visible to contractors you work with)
  • With Google Firebase, which provides the app's backend infrastructure (authentication, database, and storage)

Data storage and security

All data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore), which applies industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. The app is based in Canada and data is stored on Google Cloud infrastructure.

Data retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you wish to have your data deleted, please contact us at the email below and we will remove your account and associated data within 30 days.

Request account deletion

You can request deletion of your TS+ account and all associated data at any time. To do so:

  • Send an email to threestar11@gmail.com
  • Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request
  • Include the phone number associated with your account

We will delete your account and the following data within 30 days of receiving your request:

  • Your profile information (name, location, trade, billing address, GST number)
  • Your contract and project history
  • Your network connections
  • Any commendations or disputes associated with your account

Note: some data may be retained for a limited period where required by law or to resolve outstanding disputes.

Children's privacy

TS+ is intended for use by adults (18+) in a professional context. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18 years of age.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.

Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
